SID seamlessly works with most data sources. We have native integrations for the following services:
- File upload (dashboard and API)
- Text upload (dashboard and API)
- Google Drive including Google Docs, Google Sheets, and Google Slides
- Notion
- Gmail
- Salesforce
- Slack (coming soon)
- Dropbox (coming soon)

We support most files and text data.
- pdf
- docx, doc, pages
- pptx, ppt
- txt, rtf, md
- ... and many more.

Please contact us about getting started with third party integrations!
